Energy Meals

Built around the principles that work for you.

Your core principles for energy & health
  • • Oily fish 3x per week (salmon, sardines, mackerel – great for omega-3 and liver)
  • • Colour on every plate (berries, greens, varied veg for gut polyphenols)
  • • Protein at every meal (keeps energy steady and supports muscle)
  • • Liver-friendly choices (easy on the liver, pairs well with milk thistle)
  • • Slow carbs, not no carbs (oats, beans, etc. for sustained energy + gut feed)
  • • Take supplements with fat (avocado, olive oil, nuts, or oily fish improves absorption)
  • • Alcohol is the biggest lever (biggest single impact on energy, gut, liver, recovery)
  • • Feed your gut daily (different plants + fermented foods every day)

Daily Supplements

Quick reminders to support your energy and recovery.

Why these matter for energy:
